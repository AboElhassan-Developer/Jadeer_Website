About

JADEER LOGISTICS, previously known as Al Muhaidib Land Transport , provides its clientele with a full range of logistical, Marine, Terminal Solutions, Tank Management ( Maintenance and Cleaning), Freight Management at a global level covering 100 plus countries to include Sea freight , Air freight , Pan GCC transport solutions, Project Cargo management, Value added services (VAS) such as Customs brokerage, Warehousing solutions, Kitting, packing, stripping of containers and various associated services. JADEER LOGISTICS, with its 400 strong work force, is now a prominent logistics player offering end-to-end logistics solutions within the Kingdom of SAUDI ARABIA and other GCC countries such as BAHRAIN, KUWAIT, and UAE through our partners. Our Services

Land Transportation

  • At Jadeer, we provide cutting-edge land transportation solutions that redefine supply chain efficiency.
  • With a fleet of over 500 trucks and 400+ versatile trailers, we offer unparalleled flexibility and reliable coverage across Saudi Arabia and the GCC.
  • Every shipment is protected with comprehensive transit insurance, ensuring your business operates with peace of mind and confidence.
  • Our state-of-the-art 25,000 m² maintenance facility, supported by a team of 40+ certified technicians, minimizes downtime and maximizes operational performance.
  • For temperature-sensitive cargo, our 75+ integrated gensets guarantee uninterrupted cold chain continuity from start to finish.
  • This fully integrated approach not only ensures consistent, high-performance logistics but also offers scalable solutions that drive growth, maintain full regulatory compliance, and elevate your business with seamless, dependable service.

New ISO/Road Tank Management Depot

  • Our ISO/Road Tank Management Depot is equipped with a state-of-the-art, fully automated tank cleaning facility, adhering to the highest industry standards.
  • We specialize in cleaning a wide range of products, including industrial chemicals, food-grade materials, and more, ensuring that every tank is thoroughly cleaned to maintain optimal safety and efficiency.
  • Our comprehensive service offering includes road tanker cleaning, nitrogen blanketing, expert repairs for ISO tank shells, and efficient residual disposal.
  • Additionally, we provide full tank maintenance and cleaning services, coupled with detailed inspection and testing to preserve tank integrity and ensure strict compliance with industry regulations.
  • Partnering with us guarantees that your tanks are always ready for operation, meticulously maintained with precision and care.

Terminal Operations

  • Strategically located in Dammam, Jubail, and Jeddah, our terminal operations span 300,000 m², enabling us to handle large volumes of cargo with exceptional efficiency.
  • We offer a full range of services, including dry container storage, dry cargo and bulk storage, and reefer container storage with plug-in facilities.
  • Our cargo stuffing and de-stuffing processes enhance operational efficiency, minimizing time spent handling goods.
  • We focus on maximizing resource utilization through container lifecycle management, inventory optimization, and reducing operational downtime.
  • Our services also include container maintenance and repair, as well as comprehensive container control and inventory management, improving supply chain transparency.
  • These solutions not only help reduce costs and boost profitability but also provide you with a competitive edge in the market, ensuring your logistics needs are managed with the highest standards of reliability and performance.

Freight Services

  • Jadeer offers seamless air, sea, and inland freight services, connecting your business to global markets with efficiency and reliability.
  • Our expert team ensures smooth customs clearance, eliminating barriers to international trade.
  • Whether handling large projects or urgent charters, we provide tailored solutions to meet your needs.
  • With our global network, we offer flexible shipping options—breakbulk, LCL, and FCL—ensuring timely, cost-effective deliveries.
  • For critical shipments, we provide AOG and express services, guaranteeing on-time arrival.
  • Partner with Jadeer for unmatched reliability and a global reach that keeps your business competitive.

New Warehousing & Distribution

  • At Jadeer, we streamline your supply chain with efficient warehousing and distribution solutions.
  • We ensure your goods are always available with precise storage, inventory management, and fast order fulfillment.
  • Our cross-docking services reduce storage time, speeding up distribution and cutting costs.
  • We offer customized distribution centers and value-added services such as custom labeling and packaging.
  • Real-time tracking ensures transparency and on-time deliveries.
  • Our cold storage facilities protect temperature-sensitive goods, preserving their quality.
